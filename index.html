Plan V

The Plan

Plan V provides a path of hope for all of us.
Following the plan can lead to a better life, a life of meaning and a life of success.

The Steps

IPhysical Health

  • join a gym or training program
  • follow through with a goal of 2 hours a day
  • focus on hygiene, take your medicine and keep yourself clean

IISpiritual Health

  • read the bible/tanakh in a year (subsitute for a good long non-fiction book if desired)
  • compose a list of rules to abide by
  • keep a belief binder with answers to pressing questions, motivation and beliefs/quotes

IIILanguage

  • learn the language of your ancestors (eg german, polish, hebrew)
  • learn a language that will enlarge your world (eg spanish, mandarin, hindu)

IVProjects

  • consider working if it's legal and you can manage it
  • draw up a draft for a project you'll have to complete till graduation
  • start an organization or a community alliance

VEducation

  • don't necessarily aim for a 4.0, but try very hard to keep it 90+%
  • research universities you'd like to attend and find out about cost, admissions and scolarships
